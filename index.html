Our Mission:

The goal of this organization is to enhance the community through support of the local businesses, revitalization and beautification of main street, creation of a pleasant shopping environment for our local families while encouraging the development and preserving the revival and historic character of the community and our residents.

Sewickley Ave circa 1947

The Ocean Mining Company founded the town of Herminie in 1893 when it opened its first mine in this area. The mine and area was originally called the Ocean No. 1 mine but was renamed the Herminie Mine after Herminie Berwind, the wife of Ocean Coal Company president, Charles Berwind.
